About Me

Hello everyone! I am originally from Venezuela, but I was raised in Miami,FL from a young age. I have moved multiple times accross the USA - including Madison, WI and Chicago. Oh yes, I'm also a Badger from the University of Wisconsin Madison!

I'm currently a data solutions associate director at Horizon Media and my primary client is a movie studio called STX Entertainment. I truly, and I mean truly enjoy mixing data, marketing, and entertainment into a melting pot.

I'm also an entrepreneur, clothing designer, and hip-hop music producer/engineer on my spare time. Ultimately, I just enjoy creating products from scratch and it was these hubbies that took me out of the habit of playing video games.

I enjoy traveling to new destinations, going to concerts, and my favorite cusines are Peruvian, Indian, and Japanese. I look forward to getting to know y'all!
